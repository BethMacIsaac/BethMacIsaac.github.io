Welcome to my site! Growing up I always spent my summers at my family's cottage at the beach. The cottage had one rule: no TV (with a "no internet" clause later on). On sunny days, this was no problem... on rainy days I played a lot of cards.

Naturally, when the time came to write a web app one of my first thoughts was to write a card game. I intend to expand this page with more games later, but for now there is one game and the name of that game is "Higher or Lower" it is simple but weirdly addictive, when you press start a card will be drawn. Your mission, should you choose to accept it, is simply to guess if the next card will be higher or lower. Aces are always a point of contention in my experience, so they can either bet set to "high" or "low" depending of your house rules.

Have fun!
